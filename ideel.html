Ideel

Graphic Design

Overview

Ideel is a company specialized in biodegradable coffee capsules, produced by using 100% renewable energies. The capsules contain high-quality coffee and once they are used, they will decompose in compost within 6 months, increasing the fertility of the soil.

My role: I was the solo designer, collaborating directly with the owners of the company.

Project goal: Redesign the current icons on both the coffee capsule packaging and the website to better represent their brand identity and culture.

Duration: 3 weeks

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Paper & Pencil

Design Thinking Process
