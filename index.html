UX/UI Designer who translates ideas into user-friendly and inclusive designs.

Stefania Olteanu

As a UX/UI designer with a background in web development, I bring a unique combination of technical expertise and design acumen to all my projects. I am passionate about creating engaging and user-centered designs, aligning the users's needs and stakeholders alike.

My design process is a meticulously structured journey. It starts with thorough research, where I conduct both quantitative and qualitative analysis to gain deep insights. Next, I transition into the design phase, producing mockups, wireframes, and prototypes that breathe life into my ideas. Finally, the testing phase becomes paramount, as I rigorously validate the design to reveal and resolve potential issues before they advance to the product development stage.
